ProScience Ltd is a start-up encompassing academic services, consultancy, and field research specialising in biting insects.

At ProScience, we understand the impact biting insects can have on people, animals, and the environment. Our experts have the knowledge and experience to provide customised solutions to suit your specific needs. Whether you are a government agency, non-profit organization, or private business, we can provide you with the expertise and insights you need to tackle your biting insect-related challenges.

Our range of services includes:

Consultancy: Our experienced consultants can provide you with expert advice on all aspects of biting insect management, from identification to management options.

Field Research: We conduct cutting-edge field research to provide our clients with the latest insights and best practices for controlling biting insects. Our field research team is well-equipped to conduct field surveys, experiments, repellent trials and assessments.

Academic Expertise: Our team of experts has extensive knowledge and experience in biting insect biology, ecology, and vector-borne diseases. We can provide academic support encompassing knowledge exchange, student placements, grant applications and collaborative partnerships.

We specialise in a range of biting insects including mosquitoes, horseflies, stable flies, Culicoides biting midges and associated diseases. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest research to provide our clients with innovative solutions to the challenges posed by biting insects. At ProScience, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and client satisfaction. We work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and provide customised solutions to meet those needs. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you tackle your biting insect-related challenges.
